01 · About

Passion, Perseverance
& Purpose

Dr. Mallika Kumar is a Professor of Economics and Founder Coordinator of the Office of International Programmes (OIP) at Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), University of Delhi. She holds a Ph.D. from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, and completed the Professional Development Course at Harvard University on 'Managing Yourself and Leading Others'.

With 33 years of teaching experience, her research spans Cooperatives, Women Empowerment, and Sustainable Development Goals. She has represented India on global platforms — from speaking at the United Nations Headquarters, New York for the Soft Launch of the International Year of Cooperatives 2025, to delivering a plenary at the Centenary Cooperative Conference in Rochdale, UK — the birthplace of the cooperative movement.

Her book Cooperatives in BIMSTEC Countries was released by the Minister of Cooperation, Uttarakhand (2022). She has initiated and finalized 14 international MoUs and expanded SRCC's global reach to over 50 countries. She is strongly driven by the belief that "passion, perseverance and purpose" are the keys to meaningful impact.
